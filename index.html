Healthcare for All

Offers affordable health care in Orange County, through a network of 34+ providers.

Mid-fi Prototype Under Development

Visit Live Site / Meet the Team →

!The Challenge

The center relied on a manual, fragmented process to track and manage daily provider appointment goals. This led to high cognitive load for staff, potential data integrity issues, and overscheduling that affected patient care quality.

âœ“The Solution

A scalable, centralized platform featuring a provider directory and real-time quota tracking. We utilized heuristic evaluation to ensure the interface is intuitive for non-technical staff, streamlining operations and ensuring compliance.

Quota Management System

Key UX Decision: To reduce context switching, we implemented a contextual sidebar for quota creation. Admins can view monthly goals at a glance and adjust quotas without leaving the master list view.

Real-time data visualization for monthly quotas.

Slide-over panel for quick actions.

Provider Directory & Profiles

A centralized directory allows for quick lookup of provider details. The information architecture was restructured to prioritize critical status info (e.g., active/inactive) and contact details.

Master list with status indicators and search.

Detailed view with edit capabilities.

Design Impact

By digitizing this workflow, we anticipate a 40% reduction in scheduling errors and a significant decrease in administrative time spent on manual tracking.
